About Me

Mom. Wife. Friend. Keeper of pet birds. Lover of baked goods. Magician in training.

Mine is an interesting story with some twist and turns and maybe a dragon along the way. I’ve always loved art and would find time to doodle or create some masterpiece even at an early age. Going to college I concentrated on obtaining a degree in Biology as, according to some, doing anything with the word “art” in in was an immediate one way ticket to a life of the starving artist. After some time past, and I came to my senses of the direction I wanted in life, I enrolled in evening courses and achieved a Masters Certificate in Visual Communications and 3D Design through Johns Hopkins University. This choice paved the way for opportunities within the graphic design realm and my life quickly became better.

However, I soon realized that design, like so many things, quickly changes and certain skillsets would need to be honed or learned. When I first heard about UX and UI, it dawned on me how this may be the perfect path that merged both my skills in biology and graphic design and create the ultimate fighting force against the evils…I mean…and further enhance what I knew while staying on top of current design trends and progressing in my career.

If I’m not at home doing all that cool family stuff and baking up the next batch of picture perfect cookies and cakes, I’m at work...making websites, creating surveys, posters, graphics, and the occasional animation. While not getting too much into the details, I make things functional, optimal, and exceptional for our awesome customers.
